    W05 Prove: Reflection

    • Due Saturday by 11:59pm
    • Points 50
    • Submitting a file upload
    • File Types doc and docx
    • Available after Jan 22 at 12am

    Overview

    In this activity, you will complete a Reflection and Synthesis Report document. The purpose is to reflect and synthesize on the experiences and insights that you gained this week to deepen your understanding. Your instructor will grade your submission and provide feedback.

    Instructions

    1. Complete the Prepare: Personal Study assignment and Study Group Insights for the week before beginning this activity.
    2. Reflect on your experience and insights that you gained this week.
    3. Download the Reflection and Synthesis Report template Download Reflection and Synthesis Report template and follow the instructions on the document to complete each section of the report.
    4. Submit your report with all sections complete.
